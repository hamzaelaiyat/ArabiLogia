# Design Context

## Users

Egyptian high school students (Grades 10-12, ages 15-18) preparing for Arabic language exams. Users expect:
- Fast, focused exam interactions without distractions
- Clear visual hierarchy for Arabic text (RTL support critical)
- Encouraging feedback without childish aesthetics
- Mobile-first responsive experience

## Brand Personality

**Proud, Scholarly, Focused** — An app that respects Arabic linguistic heritage while providing modern exam prep. The brand projects cultural confidence and academic seriousness without being stuffy.

## Emotional Goals

Motivating and confidence-building. Students should feel capable and encouraged, not overwhelmed or patronized. Progress visualization should create a sense of achievement.

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Style:** Modern minimalist with warm Arabic warmth
- **Mood:** Focused, scholarly, culturally grounded
- **References:** Distraction-free learning apps (Linear, Notion-style focus)
- **Anti-references:** Cluttered traditional ed-tech, generic Western apps

## Theme

- **Primary Color:** Warm Amber (#EB8A00) — culturally resonant, energetic
- **Background:** Clean light (#F7FCFF) / Dark mode (#191B1D)
- **Typography:** ReadexPro (headings), Rubik (body) — modern Arabic-friendly
- **Glassmorphism:** Subtle frosted glass on auth screens only
- **Borders:** Soft, rounded (radiusFull for inputs/buttons)
- **Shadows:** Minimal, soft glows on primary CTAs only

## Design Principles

1. **Focus First:** Every element earns its place. No decorative clutter.
2. **Arabic Authenticity:** RTL by default. Arabic typography at scale. Respect the script.
3. **Warm Authority:** Amber suggests energy; generous spacing suggests confidence.
4. **Progressive Encouragement:** Motivating without gamification overkill. Celebrate progress meaningfully.
5. **Clarity at Speed:** Students on mobile, often nervous. Touch targets ≥ 44px. Clear feedback states.

## Component Guidelines

### Colors (in code)
- Primary: `AppColors.primary` (#EB8A00)
- Background: `AppColors.background(context)`
- Foreground: `AppColors.foreground(context)`
- Surface: `AppColors.surface(context)`
- Muted: `AppColors.mutedColor(context)`

### Spacing Scale (in code)
- Mobile content padding: 16px
- Desktop content padding: 24px
- Component gaps: 8-16px
- Section gaps: 24-32px

### Typography (in code)
- Display/Headings: `ReadexPro`, bold weight
- Body/Labels: `Rubik`, regular/medium weight
- Scale via `AppTokens.fontSize*` constants

### Elevation
- Subtle only. Primary buttons may have soft glow (box-shadow with primary color at 30% opacity).
- Cards use `AppColors.surface(context)` — no drop shadows.

### Radius
- Inputs/Buttons: Full (pill-shaped) — `radiusFull`
- Cards: `radiusMd` to `radiusLg`
- Avoid sharp corners except where structurally necessary

### Accessibility
- WCAG A compliance minimum
- Touch targets: 44px minimum
- Color contrast: Primary amber (#EB8A00) on light backgrounds needs evaluation
- No information conveyed by color alone (always pair with icons/labels)

## Anti-Patterns

- Do NOT use drop shadows on cards (use surface color difference)
- Do NOT add decorative gradients to dashboard content areas (auth is exception)
- Do NOT use emoji in UI (Arabic interface, use icons or text)
- Do NOT make anything feel childish or playful — this is serious exam prep
- Do NOT use generic Arabic-Latin mixed typography — Arabic text takes precedence
